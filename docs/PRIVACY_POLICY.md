# GoAxonAI Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-01_

This Privacy Policy explains how GoAxonAI collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website, dashboard, and related services (including AxonApply™).

## 1) Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

- **Account information**: email, password (for local accounts), name, authentication provider.
- **Profile information**: details you choose to provide for job matching and AxonApply™ form filling (e.g., phone, location, education, experience, links).
- **Resume information**: if you upload a resume for matching, we may store extracted resume text and timestamps.
- **Payment-related information**: if you purchase a plan, we may store transaction identifiers you submit (e.g., UTR), the plan selected, and verification status.
- **Usage & diagnostics**: basic logs/metadata used to operate and secure the service (e.g., request timestamps, error reports). We do not intentionally collect sensitive credentials.

## 2) How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide core features (login, dashboard, saved jobs, AI tools, extension connectivity)
- Personalize experiences (job matching, profile-based autofill where enabled)
- Maintain security, prevent abuse, and enforce plan limits
- Verify subscriptions and plan access
- Provide support and respond to inquiries

## 3) Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited data with service providers (e.g., hosting, monitoring, email) strictly to operate GoAxonAI. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the platform from fraud or abuse.

## 4) Cookies & Local Storage

We may use cookies and browser storage to keep you signed in and to support basic site functionality. You can clear cookies/storage in your browser settings; doing so may sign you out.

## 5) Data Retention

We retain information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

You can request account deletion by contacting us.

## 6) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 7) Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact: support@goaxonai.in
